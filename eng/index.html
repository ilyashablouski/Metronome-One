Description

﻿Mobile application Metronome ONE is created for musicians (guitar, drums and other music players)


It helps:

— to learn playing by click

— to be prepared for the concerts

— built-in playlists can be used on life performances.


User-friendly interface.

Clear and pleasant click sounds without headaches.

Common clicks from popular DAW.


Meet super accurate metronome with clear and pleasant clicks! This application can be used by professional musicians as well as amateurs. You can find recognizable clicks of popular DAW inside.


The metronome is created for developing your rhythm skills. It will help you during sole rehearsals or preparation for performances and concerts. By using the app you can save settings of songs and set up their sequence.


Metronome will help you work out difficult passages in an even pace, develop groove and become a more professional musician.


Metronome features:


***Interface***

Modern interface with contrast controls.

Suitable for people with different color perception.


***Sound Sets***

9 options of clicks, including recognizable sounds from popular DAW.

Each click is designed in a special way to have a clear transient and equal entrance time.

At the same time, it is well-balanced for making soft sound and being pleasant for our ear


*** Click sync ***

Supports Ableton Link - you can sync over Wi-Fi with various devices that support Ableton Link, for example, with Ableton Live.
